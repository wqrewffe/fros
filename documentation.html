Getting Started

Welcome to NafAi! This documentation will help you understand how to use our AI assistant effectively.

Quick Start Guide

  • Type your question in the search bar
  • Press Enter or click the Ask button
  • Wait for the AI to process your request
  • View the response in the chat window

Features

Global Search

NafAi can search and provide information on a wide range of topics. Simply type your question naturally.

Latest News

Use the keyword "news" followed by your topic of interest to get the latest updates.

Mathematical Calculations

Use keywords like "solve", "calculate", or "compute" followed by your mathematical expression.

Weather Information

Type "weather" followed by a location to get current weather conditions.

Tips & Tricks

  • Be specific in your questions for better results
  • Use natural language - no need for special commands
  • Check the history panel for previous conversations
  • Use the settings panel to customize your experience

Customization

NafAi offers several customization options:

  • Dark/Light mode toggle
  • Font size adjustment
  • Response speed control
  • Animation preferences
