Getting Started

How to Use NafAi

Simply type your question in the search bar and press Enter or click the Ask button (which appears when you enter text). NafAi will process your request and provide a response.

Basic Features

Explore features like theme switching, history tracking, and customizable settings to enhance your experience.

Features & Capabilities

Question Types

You can ask questions about various topics including general knowledge, news, weather, mathematical calculations, and more.

Customization

Access settings to adjust theme, font size, response speed, and animation preferences.

Account & History

Chat History

View your previous conversations by clicking the History button. Clear your history using the Clear History button.

Data Privacy

Your conversations are stored locally and can be cleared at any time. We respect your privacy.
